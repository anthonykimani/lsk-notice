FREE WEBINAR - MANAGING A LAW FIRM: SETTING UP, MANAGEMENT AND EXPECTIONS

The Council through the Young Lawyers Committee chaired by Byron Menezes, Council Member, has organized a free webinar on Managing a Law Firm: Setting Up, Management and Expectations.

The Webinar seeks to identify the different needs, skill set and obligations in running a law firm, the basic elements that distinguish lawyers who set up law firms which succeed from those that that do not take off, with a focus on client care, billing, record management, financial planning. In addition, the Panelist will dissect the financial element of setting up law firms, that dissuades even the most enterprising minds from establishing their own firms and lastly, establishing a niche area.

To this end, the Young Lawyers Committee invites members to a free webinar scheduled on Thursday, 19th January, 2023 , from 2:00pm-4:00pm. Our panelist will be Peter Oiboo Morintat, Advocate.
